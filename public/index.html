SignQuest transforms the Seder table from a recitation into a shared quest. Instead of following along, every person at the table becomes part of the story — reading clues, debating answers, and laughing together at the absurd signs left behind by history's most dramatic desert crossing.

Tradition, Humor & Discovery — All at Once

The interactive Passover Seder game is designed to feel native to the evening. Each sign puzzle is rooted in familiar Passover moments — but twisted with wit, anachronistic logic, and playful misdirection. The humor lands because the tradition is already in the room. Every Generation Gets a Role

Grandparents read clues aloud with theatrical gravitas. Teenagers spot the visual jokes hidden in the signage. Children shout out answers before anyone else. The SignQuest Passover game is memorable precisely because it doesn't flatten the table — it amplifies every voice. Group riddles reward collective thinking, and the shared moments of discovery become the stories retold for years after the Seder ends.
